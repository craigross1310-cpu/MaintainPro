# MaintainPro Marketing Plan

## Executive Summary

**Product:** MaintainPro - Maintenance Management System  
**Target Market:** Food production facilities (small to enterprise)  
**Key Differentiator:** Food safety compliance + modern UX + integrated safety permits  
**Primary Competitors:** Maintenance Planner, Shires Pirana System  
**Goal:** Capture 5% market share in UK food manufacturing within 18 months

- Lacks modern safety compliance features
- No integrated permit system

**Our Advantage:**
✅ Modern, intuitive interface  
✅ Mobile-first design  
✅ 10-minute setup vs. days  
✅ Built-in safety compliance  
✅ Integrated hot work & contractor permits

## 5. Sales Strategy

### **Sales Process:**

**Stage 1: Lead Generation (Marketing)**
- Trade shows, ads, content, etc.
- Capture in CRM

**Stage 2: Qualification (SDR/Marketing)**
- Size: 20+ employees
- Industry: Food manufacturing
- Pain: Compliance issues or old system
- Budget: Can afford £2,000+/month
- Timeline: Needs solution within 6 months

**Stage 3: Discovery Call (Sales)**
- 30 minutes
- Understand current process
- Identify pain points
- Qualify fit
- Book full demo

**Stage 4: Product Demo (Sales)**
- 60 minutes
- Custom demo showing their use cases
- Live system walkthrough
- Answer questions
- Discuss pricing

**Stage 5: Proposal (Sales)**
- Custom proposal PDF
- Pricing options (1-year, 3-year)
- Implementation timeline
- ROI calculation
- Case studies from similar companies

**Stage 6: Negotiation (Sales + Manager)**
- Address concerns
- Adjust pricing if needed
- Offer pilot program
- Set start date

**Stage 7: Close (Sales + CEO)**
- Contract signing
- Payment setup
- Onboarding scheduled

**Stage 8: Onboarding (Customer Success)**
- Kickoff call
- Data migration help
- Training sessions
- First 90 days check-ins
