## Ad Testing Strategy

### **A/B Tests to Run:**

**Test 1: Headline Focus**
- Version A: Feature-focused ("Mobile-First CMMS")
- Version B: Benefit-focused ("Reduce Downtime 40%")
- Version C: Pain-point focused ("Tired of Maintenance Planner?")

**Test 2: Visual Style**
- Version A: Product screenshots
- Version B: Happy customers
- Version C: Data/statistics graphics

**Test 3: CTA**
- Version A: "Start Free Trial"
- Version B: "See Demo Video"
- Version C: "Calculate Your Savings"

**Test 4: Offer**
- Version A: Free trial
- Version B: Free consultation
- Version C: Free audit checklist

**Run each test for minimum 500 clicks before declaring winner**

## Ad Copy Guidelines

### **Do's:**
✅ Use specific numbers (£80,000 saved, 40% reduction)
✅ Name competitors directly
✅ Include customer quotes/proof
✅ Mobile-first language
✅ Clear, singular CTA
✅ Time-specific offers
✅ Industry-specific terms

### **Don'ts:**
❌ Generic claims ("best CMMS")
❌ Tech jargon without context
❌ Multiple CTAs
❌ Vague benefits
❌ Negative competitor bashing (be factual)
❌ Too much text
❌ Unrealistic promises
