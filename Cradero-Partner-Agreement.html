1 Introduction

This document outlines the terms of a Referral Partner Agreement between Cradero ("the Company") and the undersigned referral partner ("the Partner") for the purpose of client acquisition and revenue sharing.

Cradero is a cloud-based Computerised Maintenance Management System (CMMS) designed for manufacturing, facilities management, and industrial maintenance teams. The Partner will identify, introduce, and help onboard prospective clients in exchange for an ongoing revenue share.

Key Principle: The Partner earns a recurring commission for every client they bring on board, for as long as that client remains an active paying subscriber. This is not a one-time referral fee — it is an ongoing revenue share that rewards long-term partnership.

3 Revenue Share Structure

Item Detail
Commission Rate40% of collected monthly revenue per referred client

Payment FrequencyMonthly, within 14 days of the end of each calendar month
Payment MethodBank transfer (details to be provided by Partner)
Minimum Payout£50 — balances below this roll over to the following month
Attribution Window90 days from initial introduction to signed subscription

Example: You refer a manufacturing company with 8 engineers who sign up for the Pro plan at £39/user/month.

Monthly revenue = 8 × £39 = £312

Your monthly commission = £312 × 40% = £124.80/month, every month.

5 Partner Responsibilities

  1. Identify and introduce prospective clients who would benefit from a CMMS solution
  2. Facilitate introductions between the prospective client and Cradero for product demonstrations
  3. Represent the product accurately — do not make claims about features, pricing, or capabilities that are not documented
  4. Report referrals promptly via the agreed channel (email or shared tracker) including company name, contact person, and contact details
  5. Maintain professional conduct that reflects positively on the Cradero brand

6 Cradero Responsibilities

  1. Product delivery — provide, host, and maintain the Cradero CMMS platform
  2. Sales support — handle product demonstrations, technical questions, and client onboarding
  3. Client support — provide ongoing technical support to all active clients
  4. Transparent reporting — provide the Partner with monthly statements showing referred client subscriptions, revenue collected, and commission owed
  5. Timely payment — pay commission within 14 days of month-end
  6. Product development — continue to improve and maintain the platform

7 Intellectual Property & Ownership

All intellectual property rights in the Cradero software, including but not limited to source code, design, branding, documentation, and all associated materials, are and shall remain the sole and exclusive property of Cradero.

  • This agreement does not grant the Partner any ownership, licence, or rights to the software, codebase, or brand
  • The Partner may not resell, sublicence, copy, modify, reverse-engineer, or redistribute the software
  • The Partner may not create derivative works or competing products based on Cradero
  • Marketing materials referencing Cradero must be approved in writing prior to use
  • Upon termination of this agreement, the Partner must cease all use of Cradero branding and materials

8 Client Ownership & Attribution

  • A referral is attributed to the Partner when they provide a qualified introduction (company name + decision-maker contact) that results in a paid subscription within the 90-day attribution window
  • If a referred client cancels and later resubscribes within 6 months, the Partner retains attribution
  • If a referred client was already in active discussion with Cradero prior to the Partner's introduction, attribution will not apply — disputes will be resolved in good faith
  • The client relationship belongs to Cradero — the Partner facilitates the introduction but does not manage the client account

9 Exclusivity & Non-Compete

  • This agreement is non-exclusive — the Partner may refer clients to other products, and Cradero may engage other referral partners
  • The Partner agrees not to actively direct referred Cradero clients toward competing CMMS products for the duration of this agreement
  • The Partner may refer clients in any industry or geography unless otherwise agreed in writing

11 Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all non-public information shared under this agreement, including but not limited to:

  • Pricing structures and commission rates
  • Client lists and contact information
  • Business strategies and financial information
  • Technical details of the software platform

This obligation survives termination of the agreement for a period of 2 years.

12 General Provisions

  • Independent Contractor: The Partner is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, or legal representative of Cradero. The Partner has no authority to bind Cradero to any obligation.
  • Amendments: This agreement may only be amended in writing, signed by both parties.
  • Entire Agreement: This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, or agreements.
  • Severability: If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force.
  • Governing Law: This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which Cradero operates.
  • Dispute Resolution: Any disputes shall first be addressed through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes may be escalated to mediation or arbitration.
